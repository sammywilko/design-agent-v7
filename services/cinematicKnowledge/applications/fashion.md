# Fashion Content Application Guide

## Content Definition
Fashion content includes editorial, lookbooks, campaign imagery, runway coverage, e-commerce, and lifestyle fashion content. Bridges between pure product showcase and aspirational storytelling.

---

## KEY VISUAL PRINCIPLES

### Primary Goals
1. **Product showcase** - garment is the star
2. **Aspirational beauty** - desire creation
3. **Brand identity** - visual language consistency
4. **Lifestyle aspiration** - viewer wants this life

---

## SHOT TYPE APPLICATIONS

### ECU (Extreme Close-Up)
- **Texture showcase** - fabric quality, weave, material
- **Jewelry detail** - craftsmanship, design
- **Makeup artistry** - beauty detail, technique
- **Material luxury** - leather, silk, cashmere texture
- **Button/hardware** - quality details

### CU (Close-Up)
- **Beauty close-up** - face, makeup, skin
- **Detail emphasis** - jewelry, accessories
- **Emotional fashion** - feeling behind the look
- **Model expression** - mood and attitude
- **Hair detail** - styling showcase

### MCU (Medium Close-Up)
- **Beauty focus** - face and styling together
- **Jewelry/accessory** - detail with context
- **Editorial portrait** - fashion beauty standard
- **Upper body fashion** - neckline, shoulders
- **Headwear** - hats, headpieces

### MS (Medium Shot)
- **Upper body fashion** - tops, jackets, accessories
- **Face + outfit** - beauty and style together
- **Jewelry/accessories** - detail with context
- **Standard editorial** - most common fashion shot
- **Layering showcase** - how pieces work together

### MWS (Medium Wide Shot)
- **Outfit emphasis** - more detail than WS
- **Style showcase** - outfit with personality
- **Editorial pose** - fashion stance, attitude
- **Pants/skirts** - lower body emphasis
- **Bag/accessory** - in context with outfit

### WS (Wide Shot)
- **Full outfit showcase** - head-to-toe look
- **Silhouette** - garment shape and drape
- **Movement** - how clothes move, flow
- **Styling context** - complete look with accessories
- **Runway standard** - full look visible

### EWS (Extreme Wide Shot)
- **Location fashion** - model in environment
- **Lifestyle context** - aspirational setting
- **Editorial landscape** - fashion meets nature/city
- **Campaign imagery** - brand world building

---

## CAMERA ANGLE APPLICATIONS

### Low Angle
- **Aspirational beauty** - model as ideal
- **Editorial power** - high-fashion dominance
- **Runway perspective** - audience looking up
- **Luxury positioning** - premium aspiration
- **Dramatic editorial** - avant-garde

### Eye Level
- **Relatable luxury** - accessible premium
- **Lifestyle connection** - viewer could be there
- **Product authenticity** - honest presentation
- **Commercial fashion** - approachable
- **E-commerce standard** - accurate representation

### High Angle
- **Editorial perspective** - artistic choice
- **Pattern/texture emphasis** - showing fabric design
- **Vulnerable beauty** - intimate, exposed
- **Flat lay** - product arrangement
- **Accessory detail** - looking down on items

### Dutch Angle
- **Edgy/avant-garde** - high-fashion editorial
- **Dynamic energy** - movement and action
- **Rule-breaking** - fashion-forward statement
- **Music/campaign** - energy and attitude
- **Use thoughtfully** - matches brand if edgy

### Overhead
- **Pattern/design** - fabric construction visible
- **Flat lay aesthetic** - product arrangement
- **Editorial art** - unusual perspective
- **Accessory arrangement** - styled collection

## LIGHTING FOR FASHION

### High Key
- **Clean beauty** - flawless, perfect
- **Commercial fashion** - accessible luxury
- **Product clarity** - seeing the garment
- **E-commerce** - accurate representation
- **Fresh/modern** - contemporary aesthetic

### Low Key
- **High fashion editorial** - sophisticated, artistic
- **Luxury positioning** - premium, exclusive
- **Dramatic beauty** - sculptural, striking
- **Evening/formal** - occasion wear
- **Artistic statement** - fashion as art

### Soft Light
- **Beauty photography** - flawless skin
- **Product quality** - fabric and texture visible
- **Flattering portraits** - model at best
- **Commercial standard** - accessible beauty
- **Romantic mood** - soft aesthetic

### Hard Light
- **High fashion edge** - dramatic, artistic
- **Editorial drama** - magazine aesthetic
- **Texture emphasis** - material quality
- **Avant-garde** - bold statement
- **Summer/outdoor** - harsh sun aesthetic

### Golden Hour
- **Lifestyle fashion** - warm, aspirational
- **Campaign imagery** - premium feeling
- **Natural beauty** - sun-kissed
- **Editorial warmth** - romantic, dreamy

### Color Temperature
- **Neutral** - accurate garment color (critical for sales)
- **Warm** - golden beauty, romantic
- **Cool** - avant-garde, futuristic

## BEAT STRUCTURE FOR FASHION

### Editorial Spread Structure
1. **Hero shot** - main look, strongest image
2. **Detail shots** - texture, accessories
3. **Environment** - lifestyle context
4. **Movement** - dynamic, energy
5. **Close beauty** - face, makeup

### Campaign Structure
1. **Establishing** - brand world, location
2. **Hero product** - key item showcase
3. **Lifestyle** - product in aspirational context
4. **Detail** - quality, craftsmanship
5. **Beauty** - model/brand ambassador

### Lookbook Structure
1. Full look (WS) - complete outfit
2. Upper body (MS) - face and top
3. Lower body (MWS) - bottom and shoes
4. Details (ECU) - accessories, texture
5. Back view (WS) - reverse if needed

---

## STYLE CATEGORIES

### High Fashion / Editorial
- Dramatic lighting (low key, hard)
- Strong angles (low, dutch)
- Artistic composition
- Statement imagery
- Bold, provocative

### Commercial / Accessible
- Clean lighting (high key, soft)
- Eye level, relatable
- Clear product visibility
- Lifestyle integration
- Aspirational but attainable

### Luxury
- Sophisticated lighting (mixed)
- Premium angles (slight low)
- Quality emphasis
- Environmental context
- Exclusive feeling

### E-Commerce
- Neutral, accurate lighting
- Eye level, standard angles
- Product accuracy priority
- Clean, distraction-free
- Multiple angles coverage

### Street / Casual
- Natural lighting
- Documentary feel
- Movement and energy
- Urban environments
- Authentic, unstaged feeling

---

## PLAYFUL/COMEDY IN FASHION

### When to Use
- Behind-the-scenes content
- Social media personality
- Playful brand identity
- Model personality content

### Key Timing Rules
- Reaction shots held 2-3 seconds
- Pattern establish then break (pose, pose, silly)
- Natural moments between poses
- Authentic personality over forced humor

---

## PRODUCTION CONSIDERATIONS

### Priority Coverage
1. **Full look** - head-to-toe (multiple angles)
2. **Face/beauty** - model close-up
3. **Product detail** - key garment features
4. **Movement** - how it moves/drapes
5. **Lifestyle** - context and environment

### E-Commerce Requirements
- Front view (WS)
- Back view (WS)
- Side views (MWS)
- Detail shots (ECU)
- On-model and flat lay

### Consistency Priorities
- **Color accuracy** - garment colors true
- **Model appearance** - consistent across shots
- **Lighting continuity** - same setup for collection
- **Brand aesthetic** - matches brand identity

### Common Challenges
- Color accuracy across lighting conditions
- Garment wrinkles and fit issues
- Model energy consistency
- Weather for outdoor shoots
- Time constraints for multiple looks

## EXAMPLE SHOT BREAKDOWNS

### Editorial Fashion Shoot
1. EWS - Model in environment (4s)
2. WS - Full look, dramatic pose (3s)
3. Low angle WS - Powerful stance (3s)
4. MS - Upper body, expression (3s)
5. MCU - Beauty, makeup focus (2s)
6. ECU - Texture detail (2s)
7. WS - Movement shot (3s)

### E-Commerce Product
1. WS front - Full look, neutral pose (3s)
2. WS back - Reverse view (3s)
3. MWS - 3/4 angle (3s)
4. MS - Upper body detail (2s)
5. ECU - Fabric close-up (2s)
6. ECU - Hardware/details (2s)
7. Flat lay - Product only (2s)

### Campaign/Brand Content
1. EWS - Location establishing (4s)
2. WS - Model arrives in environment (3s)
3. MS - Lifestyle moment (3s)
4. MCU - Emotional connection (2s)
5. ECU - Product detail (2s)
6. WS - Hero shot, aspirational (4s)
7. End card/brand moment
